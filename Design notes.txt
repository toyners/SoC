Player holds counts for resources, list of settlement/city locations, list of development cards
PlayerView holds number of resource cards, number of hidden and unhidden development cards

BoardView holds information on locations and trails for purposes of display. Includes robber location.
- Verifies the settlement placement before passing to GameManager
- Verifies the road placement before passsing to GameManager
- Verifies the city placement before passing to GameManager

Board holds all locations and roads and resource providers
- Verifies the settlement placement before updating the board
- Updates the board with a new settlement for a player
- Verifies the road placement before updating the board
- Updates the board with a new road for a player
- Verifies the city placement before updating the board
- Updates the board with a new city for a player
- Returns the resources manifest for a given dice roll to the GameManager

GameManager manages player turns and interactions between the players, the board, the boardview
- At start of each player turn performs dice roll, collects resource manifest from board based on dice roll, and passes resources back to players.

GameController
- is the channel from the client to the GameManager (GM could be local or on a server). It passes through commands and passes back updated BoardView and/or PlayerView
- Also handles the game setup conversation between client and GM
